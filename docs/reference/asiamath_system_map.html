Module reference
Public layer
🌐
M1Public Portal
The network's front door. Lists member and observer institutes, activities, and open application calls. Surfaces conference microsites (M2), prize archives (M6), school listings (M8), and the outreach section (M13).
Open accessNo login required
📬
M5Newsletter & Communications
Periodic newsletter distributed to network members and subscribers. Content fed from conferences (M2), schools (M8), and prize announcements (M6). Public archive of past issues. Member institutes can submit news items. Inspired by IMU-Net and CIMPA Newsletter.
Public subscriptionEmail dispatchArchive
📹
M9Video Library
Open-access repository of recorded talks, mini-courses, and public lectures from network conferences (M2) and schools (M8). Searchable by speaker, topic, and MSC code. Speaker consent managed at point of upload. Inspired by CIRM's audiovisual mathematics archive.
Open accessMSC searchConsent managed
📰
M12Publications & Preprints
Open-access repository receiving conference reports (M2) and school lecture notes/proceedings (M8). DOI assignment, searchable by author/topic/MSC code. Includes an accessible "Snapshots" article series for non-specialist readers, modelled on Oberwolfach Reports and MFO Snapshots of Modern Mathematics.
Open accessDOISnapshots series
✨
M13Mathematics Outreach
Public-facing mathematical content promoting mathematics to broader audiences. Multilingual accessible articles, International Day of Mathematics (March 14) coordination across member institutes, and media kits. Surfaced through the public portal (M1). Inspired by the IMU Leelavati Prize model and MFO Snapshots series.
MultilingualIDM coordinationOpen access
Scientific programs
📋
M2Conference Organisation System
Full conference management: admin setup, organiser tools, participant registration, travel plans, talk submissions, programme builder, and auto-generated conference microsite. Advanced feature: Oberwolfach-style dining seating facilitation — participants privately indicate meeting preferences; system helps organisers create matched seating arrangements for meals. Outputs recordings to M9, reports to M12, news to M5.
AuthenticatedProgramme exportOberwolfach diningMicrosite generation
🏆
M6Prizes & Awards
Network-level prizes recognising mathematical achievement in the Asian region. Online nomination forms with supporting documents. Confidential selection committee workspace with conflict-of-interest management. Public archive of past laureates. Prize types may include: young researcher prize, distinguished mathematician prize, outreach prize, mathematics education prize. Modelled on IMU prizes and CIMPA Ibni Prize.
NominationsConfidential reviewConflict-of-interestPublic archive
✈
M7Travel Grants & Fellowships
Supports researcher mobility within the network. Grant types: conference travel grants, visiting researcher fellowships (short stays), Research in Pairs/Groups (2–4 researchers, 2–4 weeks, possibly split across two member institutes), and early career/PhD support. Reuses application infrastructure from M3. Post-visit report submission required. Modelled on ICTP Visiting Fellowships, ERCOM IRiGs, IMU-Simons Travel Fellowships, and MFO Research in Pairs.
Research in PairsEarly careerPost-visit report
🎓
M8Schools & Training Programs
Support for summer schools, research schools, and doctoral training events. Distinct from conferences — pedagogical format targeting early career researchers. Program types: research schools (CIMPA-style), PhD summer schools, Arbeitsgemeinschaft study groups, mini-courses by invited experts. School proposals submitted by member institutes. Student application and financial support managed. Outputs recordings to M9, lecture notes to M12, news to M5. Modelled on SEAMS Schools and CIMPA Research Schools.
Proposal submissionStudent financial supportLecture materials
Administration
📄
M3Application System
Shared application infrastructure for job and position postings. Network-level postings and member-institute postings share the same applicant-facing interface. Applicants submit cover letters, research and teaching statements. Referees receive confidential upload links. Member institute admins manage their own applicant pools. Also powers travel grant applications (M7).
Job postingsReferee lettersInstitute adminShared with M7
⚖
M10Governance & Document Management
Official documents, steering committee operations, and voting for network governance bodies. Documents: statutes, bylaws, policies, meeting minutes, annual reports, strategic plans. Features: member institute login area, online voting/polling, committee member directory (private), meeting scheduling and agenda management. Governance oversight of conferences (M2), prizes (M6), and grants (M7). Modelled on ERCOM/EMS committee structure.
Members onlyOnline votingDocument repository
🤝
M14Industry & Partner Engagement
Interface for industry partners, foundations, and donors. Functions: Study Groups with Industry (ESGI-type events), industry problem submissions, expert matching using the Academic Directory (M4), sponsorship and donation management. Restricted partner portal for external collaborators. MOU and partnership document repository. Modelled on EU-MATHS-IN and ERCIM industry collaboration models.
Partner portalExpert matchingESGI eventsDonations
Core infrastructure
👥
M4Academic Directory & Expertise Registry
The cross-cutting foundation of the system. Profiles for all academics affiliated with the network: name, affiliation, position, research area tags, MSC codes, keywords, personal page / ORCID links. Serves expert lists to: programme committees (M2), application review panels (M3), prize selection committees (M6), grant evaluators (M7), and industry expert matching (M14). Conflict-of-interest filtering built in. Must be established early — every review process in the network depends on it.
